Discover Our Awesome Plans

Monthly Membership

  • Billing Cycle With a monthly membership plan, members are billed for their gym access on a recurring monthly basis.
  • Full Gym Access Monthly members typically have the same access to the gym's facilities and amenities as other membership types. This includes the use of exercise equipment.
  • Opportunity to Try the Gym: For individuals new to the gym, a monthly membership plan can serve as a trial period to experience the gym's atmosphere, equipment, and services before deciding on a more extended commitment.

Annual Membership

  • Unlimited Access Annual gym members have the advantage of unlimited access to the gym during regular operating hours.
  • Personal Training Personal trainers can provide tailored workout programs and guidance to help members achieve their specific fitness goals.
  • Fitness Classes Fitness classes, such as yoga, spinning, Zumba, strength training, and more. Only Annual members can participate in these classes
  • Cost Savings Committing to an annual membership often results in cost savings compared to paying month-to-month fees.

No-Contract Memberships

  • Monthly Billing Cycle Members are billed for their gym access on a month-to-month basis, and they have the option to renew their membership or cancel it at the end of each month.
  • Flexibility This Plan best Suits for individuals who may have uncertain schedules or anticipate changes in their fitness routine.
  • Ease of Cancellation Members can cancel their no-contract membership at any time without the hassle of fulfilling a notice period or dealing with early termination fees.
